Abstract

The Crop Characterization Ontology provides main concepts and properties for describing the characteristics of various crops. These concepts enable an easy and quick discrimination between phenotypes. They are generally highly heritable, can be easily seen by the eye and are equally expressed in all environments. In addition, these may include a limited number of additional traits thought desirable by a consensus of users of the particular crop.
